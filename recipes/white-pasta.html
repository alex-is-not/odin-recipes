Cheesy Pasta

Description

Another time, I wanted cheesy pasta but did not have time to go to the store , nor did I have a lot of fancy ingredients. So I looked and found some old ramen packets in my pantry, and some cheese I had leftover from a party that I really had. I made the pasta in the pan and liked how it turned out, hopefully you do too!

Above is the end result.

Ingredients

  • Cheese, shredded
  • Pasta, any kind you have (approximately 400 grams)
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Ramen seasoning, or any kind of seasoning you'd like
  • oil
  • garlic

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to instructions.
  2. While the pasta is boiling, mince garlic and fry lightly in oil.
  3. Stir in seasoning.
  4. Stir in sun-dried tomatoes.
  5. Once pasta is done, drain and plop unto the pan.
  6. Mix in cheese. Serve warm.
