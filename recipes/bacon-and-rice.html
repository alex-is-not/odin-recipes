Bacon and Rice

Description

Once upon a time, I wanted bacon. I went to the store and bought some bacon. I then went ahead and put the bacon in an oven rack, turning on the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. I baked the bacon until it was crispy and golden.

Above is the end result.

Ingredients

  • bacon, or pork belly is fine, 1 pound
  • white rice, cooked according to package instruction
  • 2 eggs
  • garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to instructions, and set aside.
  2. Lay out the bacon side-by-side on a flat baking tray. Try not to overlap the bacon pieces.
  3. Turn on the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Bake until bacon is in the preferred crispness, around 20 minutes.
